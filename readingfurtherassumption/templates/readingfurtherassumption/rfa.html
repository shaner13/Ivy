This calculator forecasts the output in kwh and savings in euros for solar panels in Ireland based on the location, where the kWh is converted to its value in euros based on the predicted grid demand for that month, however the cost of electricity depends on other factors which are not accounted for such as economical factors or the time of day electricity is being consumed.
As this calculator is for domestic solar panels it will forecast the output and savings based on a max installation of 6kWp as larger installations are generally for industrial purposes or farms thus retailers will offer different prices. The new Microgeneration Support Scheme is available for domestic installations up to 6kWp where excess supplied to the grid is renumerated at a competitive market rate. Learn more about this scheme.
The initial cost of the solar panels includes the SEAI Grant which is available for €1900 for 1kWp with an additional €300 per kW up to €2400 for 4kWp. Find out more about the SEAI grant here.
This calculator is only applicable for a solar PV system, wherein other technologies such as a battery or heating are not included, it also does not account for solar thermal which can be used to heat water or air. The prices for the solar panels are based off 0.25kWp increments, where 1kWp costs €1900. You can find a list of SEAI registered Solar PV companies here.
